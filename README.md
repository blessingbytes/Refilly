#  Refilly



## What Refillly Does
Refillly is a web and mobile app that connects households with trusted gas refill vendors. Users can schedule home refills for cooking gas cylinders.

## Why It Matters
Running out of gas is a major inconvenience. Many vendors operate offline, making it hard for users to locate nearby refill services. Refillly makes the process safe, reliable, and convenient, while giving vendors better reach and coordination.

- **Secondary Users:** Gas refill vendors seeking digital visibility and efficient order management.

## Key Features
- **Online Ordering & Scheduling:** Select gas type, cylinder size, and schedule a refill at your preferred time.
- **Home Refill Service:** Vendors come directly to your doorstep to refill your gas safely.
- **Nearby Vendor Locator:** Find trusted gas refill vendors in your area for quick service or emergencies.
- **Secure Payment:** Pay digitally through the app for convenience and transparency.
